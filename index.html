About Me

My name is David Levens and I am currently enrolled in a Full Stack Web Developer bootcamp at the University of Connecticut, School of Engineering at Stamford. Prior to my current training, I worked at NBC Universal for over 38 years as Senior Audio Engineer on NBC Today show as well as NBC Sports Primetime Olympic coverage from Barcelona Spain in 1992 thru London England in 2012. In that time, I have accunulated 9 Emmy awards for Olympiic Primetime coverage, and 1 very special Emmy for my personal contributions and trailblazing innovations on the NBC Today show.

I am very happily married to my wife Erica for 30 years this month, and have 2 wonderful sons. Philip graduated from the University of Pennsylvania iin 2018 and Terrence who is a senior at UPENN and will graduate in 2020.

Erica and I are also the proud parents of Picabo J Levens, our beautiful female chocolate Labrador retriever who is approaching her 14th birthday.
